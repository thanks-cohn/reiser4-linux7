# THE ENDGOAL

We prioritize stability first and then innovation.

A filesystem that cannot protect data has no right to dream, but a filesystem that only protects yesterday has no right to lead tomorrow.

The first crown is survival: clean builds, clean mounts, clean writes, clean reads, clean unmounts, clean recovery, and boring dependability under abuse.

Only after that crown is earned do we chase superiority.

The endgoal is not merely to revive Reiser4-NX, but to forge it into a filesystem so stable, inspectable, recoverable, adaptable, and intelligent that modern filesystems become the baseline it surpasses.

Dreaming of anything less is death.

Dreaming anything less is death.
